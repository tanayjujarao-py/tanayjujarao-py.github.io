What Actually Wins IPL Matches? A Ball-by-Ball Analysis

Python Pandas NumPy Matplotlib SQL Excel Tableau Jupyter
Headline Insight:

"Matches are decided quietly in the first 15 overs, not in the dramatic finish."

Analyzed ~500K+ ball-by-ball IPL deliveries across multiple seasons, segmenting every delivery into Powerplay, Middle, and Death phases to find what statistically predicts winning.

  • Toss winners win just 50.15% of matches — essentially a coin flip
  • Powerplay dominance → 67.3% win rate (batting first) / 70.8% (chasing) — vs Death Overs at just 53.2% / 50.0%
  • Winning teams preserve wickets early: 1.61 wickets lost chasing vs 2.46 for losers
  • Defending 180+: economy matters most (74.6% win rate). Below 180: wickets matter most (48.7% vs 23.5%)
  • After 3 dot balls, another dot ball is most likely (43.7%), not a wicket as fans assume
Methodology Note: Ball-by-ball IPL data, multiple seasons, Python/SQL/Excel/Tableau, phase segmentation + correlation analysis + pressure sequence analysis.
